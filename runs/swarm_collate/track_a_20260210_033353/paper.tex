\begin{abstract}
We benchmark SWARM coordination mechanisms on a verifiable reasoning track, comparing divergence, critique, reconciliation, and memory retrieval. We report accuracy, disagreement rates, and costs across 50 tasks.
\end{abstract}

\section{Introduction}
We evaluate SWARM-style coordination mechanisms on Track A (verifiable reasoning),
using controlled arithmetic and word-problem tasks with deterministic checks.
Each condition corresponds to a coordination policy (divergence, critique,
reconciliation, memory). This paper summarizes one full run (ID: track\_a\_20260210\_033353).

\section{Methods}
Tasks: 50 total, generated with fixed random seed and difficulty calibration.
