# LLM-guided evolutionary optimization of baseline governance (50 iterations)

## Design

- **Type**: Single evolutionary optimization run
- **Scenario**: baseline
- **Seed base**: 42
- **Iterations**: 50 (901 total evaluations)
- **Parents per iteration**: 3
- **Eval epochs**: 5 (final eval: 10 epochs x 10 steps)
- **LLM mutator**: claude-sonnet-4-20250514
- **Fitness weights**: low_toxicity=0.35, welfare=0.30, quality_gap=0.20, payoff_gap=0.15
